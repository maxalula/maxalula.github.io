About the Game

Before the Legends - Online is a MMORPG game featuring open world, quest system, level progression, equipment and a whole lot more.

Choose a server, create a character with a class and gender and push your character to it's limits, upgrading its level, skills and equipment

Key features include:

Online multiplayer

Character customization

Level system

Craft system

Itens and Inventory

Equipment upgrade

Pets

Skill trees


Game Profile

Development Status: Released to beta in 2016. Servers currently offline since 2019.

Engine and Programming Language: Unity, C#, Photon PUN.


My Contribuitions

This was a portifolio game, with many 3D assets from the Unity asset store. I focused on developing my game design and programming skills.

Further into it's phase 2 development, the second version and server was remade with friends from college that took care of 3d, UI and animation to make it unique.

Since it was my first contact with Multiplayer programming, it was a challenge, but also an opportunity for growth.

My role also touches game design, as in the balacing of the game; I've developed the item's effects and power levels, and balaced via playtests.
